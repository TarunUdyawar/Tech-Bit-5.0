Typing Titans

Description:

"Typing Titans" is a fast-paced typing game where participants compete to demonstrate their typing speed and accuracy. The objective is to type a given passage as quickly and precisely as possible. The participant who achieves the highest speed with the fewest errors will emerge as the winner.

Rounds:

1. First Round:
o Duration: 5 minutes
o Objective: Type the given content accurately and quickly.
o Elimination: The 5 slowest participants will be eliminated.

2. Second Round:
o Duration: 3 minutes
o Objective: Type the new content with high speed and accuracy.
o Elimination: The 5 slowest participants will be eliminated.

3. Third Round:
o Duration: 2 minutes
o Objective: Type the final content swiftly and accurately.

o Complexity: In this event, participants will be judged based on points. The total points will be equal to the number of words in the paragraph. The participant with the highest points wins the game. However, every time a participant clicks backspace, points will be deducted for each word, and if any letter is wrong, points will not be awarded for that particular letter.

o Winner: The participant with the highest points and accuracy in this round will be declared the winner

Rules:

• Solo Game
• Each round will have a different passage to type.
• Speed and accuracy will both be taken into account.
• Any typing errors will result in a penalty to the participant's score.
