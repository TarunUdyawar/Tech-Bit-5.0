About TechBit 5.0

Techbit, an extravaganza of innovation and brilliance fest hosted by the Department of BScIT, Tolani College Of Commerce (Autonomous). Embracing the spirit of progress and cutting-edge technology, Techbit is our annual intercollegiate fest that promises to be a celebration like no other.

Our Purpose

At TECHBIT 5.0, we're here to show you that technology is not only cool but also tons of fun! We want to bring together people who love tech and those who love games, and let them discover how these two worlds can create amazing things together. Our goal is to give you a chance to learn, play, and connect in a way that sparks your imagination and makes you excited about the future. Come join us and be part of this awesome adventure where tech and fun collide!

Our Vision

At TECHBIT 5.0, we want to bring together the cool world of technology and the thrill of gaming. Our aim is to show you how fun and exciting technology can be, whether you're a tech whiz or a game lover. We want to help you learn new things, make new friends, and have an awesome time exploring the amazing possibilities that tech and gaming offer. Join us in this adventure of discovery, where you can be part of shaping the way technology and fun come together!

Why attend ?

Join us for an amazing day where technology meets fun! Experience thrilling challenges, exciting games, and a chance to learn about the cool things tech can do. Whether you're into coding or just love playing games, there's something for everyone. Make new friends, explore futuristic innovations, and have a blast at Tech Bit 5.0. Don't miss this chance to be part of an unforgettable adventure!
